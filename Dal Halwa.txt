Ingredients for Moong Dal Halwa buynow


1 tin-NESTLÉ MILKMAID Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 cup-MILKMAID Pure Ghee
3 cups-Milk
1 cup (150 gm)-Moong Dal (dhuli)
1/2 tsp-Elaichi (Cardamom) Powder
3 tbsp-Badam (Almonds) cut into slivers
3 tbsp-Kishmish (Raisins)
3 tbsp-Pista chopped


How to make Moong Dal Halwa

1.Clean and soak the moong dal for 30 minutes, drain and  
 grind to a coarse paste. Fry the dal paste in ghee, till it turns golden brown.
2.Add the milk and cook on low flame, stirring 
 continuously, till milk dries up. Add the NESTLÉ MILKMAID Sweetened Condensed Milk and cook, stirring continuously, till desired consistency is reached.
3.Mix well and transfer to a bowl. Garnish with the 
 almonds. raisins and pistachio.
